About Frank

Frank Wrubel is a strategic enterprise architect specializing in unified communications, Microsoft messaging ecosystems, and enterprise infrastructure design. Throughout his career he has helped large organizations modernize communication systems, scale collaboration platforms, and improve system reliability for tens of thousands of users. His expertise spans Microsoft Exchange architecture, Skype for Business, hybrid cloud infrastructure, and enterprise migrations from legacy collaboration platforms to modern cloud solutions.
